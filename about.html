My Experience

Full Stack Developer | AuxCode

From mid 2024 to early 2025, I worked with AuxCode on a full-stack logistics platform. I helped modernize the frontend by moving 50+ old components into PrimeVue and built a quick demo site in just a few weeks to show how fast the new framework could be adopted. I also handled plenty of testing and bug fixes to keep everything running smoothly for an enterprise IoT client.

The experience taught me a lot about writing maintainable code and collaborating on production systems.
